About Dr.



Dr. Kausiki Ray Sarkar is a renowned reproductive medicine specialist of Kolkata. She is presently working as HOD, Fertility Unit, AMRI Medical centre, 97A Southern Avenue, Kolkata for last 5 years. Before this, she was in Genome Fertility centre for 8 years. After completing DGO (Calcutta) and DNB (Delhi) in Gynecology and Obstetrics, she had done fellowship and hands on training in Reproductive Endocrinology & Surgery and Assisted Reproduction at Sourasky Medical Centre, Tel Aviv, Israel. She is a senior member of different State and National bodies of Reproductive Medicine. As a Gynecologist she exclusively practices only in the field of reproductive medicine and manages the whole spectrum of fertility problems including AIH, AID, IVF, ICSI, TESA/TESE, PESA, EGG DONATION, SURROGACY, PGS, PGD, ERA as well as FERTILITY PRESERVATION. She had special fertility preservation training from Prof Foad Azam , director fertility preservation unit, Tel Aviv. Apart from full time attachment in AMRI medical centre, she conducts OPD in AMRI Dhakuria, AMRI Mukundopur and AMRI Saltlake. She is currently clinical Secretary ISAR Bengal & also immediate Past Joint Secretary IFS Bengal.
